About the Developer

The story behind these projects

Hi, my name is Subhadeep Dutta. I am 21 years old, from India, and the sole developer of these userscripts and extensions. I am not a professional developer; I simply do this as a hobby.

I developed the Firefox extension because, after Firefox stopped supporting Chrome extensions, there wasn't a good image downloader available in the Firefox store comparable to Chrome's "Image Downloader". So, I decided to build it myself. I believe it is significantly better than the Chrome alternative and fetches many more images that even the Chrome version misses.

As for the userscripts, I love reading manga, manhwa, manhua, and comics. Many people want to download images from comic reading sites, so I built these tools for them. I focus on sites that I personally use and those not currently supported on GreasyFork.

I am aware there may still be a few bugs and issues. I plan to fix these in the future. To keep me motivated, please consider making a small donation and sharing your valuable feedback.

Thank you for your support!
